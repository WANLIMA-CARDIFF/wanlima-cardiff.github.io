I am currently a PhD student in the visual computing group within the School of Computer Science & Informatics, Cardiff University. I received my MSc degree in Image and Video Communication and Signal Processing with Distinction from University of Bristol, UK, in 2020. My PhD topic is Remote Sensing Image Fusion (ReSIF) for Environmental Applications supervised by Dr. Oktay Karakus and Prof. Paul Rosin. My work involves Computer Vision, semi-supervised learning, Remote Sensing Image Fusion, Land Cover Mapping, etc.
