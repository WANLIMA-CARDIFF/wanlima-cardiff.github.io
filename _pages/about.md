I am current a PhD student in visual computing group within the school of Computer Science & Informatics, Cardiff University. I received my MSc degree in Image and Video Communication and Signal Processing from University of Bristol, UK, in 2020. My PhD thesis is Remote Sensing Image Fusion for Environmental Applications (ReSIF) supervised by Dr. Oktay Karakus and Prof. Paul Rosin. My work involves Remote Sensing Image Fusion, Semi-supervised Learning, Few-shot Learning, Land Cover Classification, etc.
