About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Engineering and an affiliated member of Clare Hall at the University of Cambridge, working on the EPSRC-funded MicroBlast project led by Prof. Samuel Rigby. I am a member of IMPACT group led by Dr. Sivasakthy Selvakumaran. Prior to joining Cambridge, I completed my PhD at Cardiff University's Visual Computing Group, led by Prof. Paul Rosin, under supervision of Dr. Oktay Karakus and Prof. Paul Rosin in 2025. I received my Master's Degree with Distinction from the University of Bristol in 2021.
